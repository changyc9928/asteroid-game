Asteroids

Level: 1

Score: 0

Life:

Instrucions:
1. Press w and s to move forward and backward.
2. Press a and d to pan to left or right.
3. Click on your mouse to fire a bullet.
4. Moving mouse in the canvas to rotate the ship.
5. The asteroids will randomly spawn anywhere within the canvas (even just on the ship, so good luck).
6. The asteroids will wrap around in this canvas if they move out of the boundaries.
7. The game will start with level 1 and 3 lifes .
8. Each succesful shot on SMALL asteroids will contribute 10 marks.
9. Large asteroids will break down into 4 and won't contribute any mark when shot.
10. The level will increase every 100 scores.
11. The level change will influence the asteroid speed, with level 1 the slowest and increasing exponentially with respect to level.
12. Collisions between ship and asteroid will cause you to lose one life.
13. The game ends when all 3 lifes have been used up.
14. It is possible to get "double kill".
